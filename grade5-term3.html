Grade 5 – Term 3 Notes

Maths – Term 3

📘 Percentages

Percentages show parts out of 100. Example: 50% = 50/100 = 1/2

📘 Converting Fractions

Fractions can be changed into percentages. Example: 1/2 = 50%

📘 Basic Algebra

Using letters to represent numbers. Example: x + 3 = 7

📘 Word Problems

If 50% of 20 learners are girls → 10 are girls

🧪 Practice

1. What is 25% of 100?
2. Convert 1/4 into a percentage
3. Solve: x + 5 = 10

🧠 Summary

- Percent means out of 100
- Fractions can convert to percentages
- Algebra uses letters for numbers

English – Term 3

📘 Writing Skills

Writing paragraphs and short essays clearly.

📘 Tenses

Past, present, and future tense.

📘 Reading

Understanding stories and answering questions.

🧪 Practice

1. Write a sentence in past tense
2. What is present tense?
3. Write 3 sentences about your school

🧠 Summary

- Use correct tense
- Write clearly
- Understand what you read

NST – Term 3

📘 Systems

Systems are groups of parts that work together.

📘 Environment

Everything around us including plants, animals, and people.

📘 Human Impact

How people affect the environment (pollution, recycling).

🧪 Practice

1. What is a system?
2. Give one example of pollution
3. How can we protect the environment?

🧠 Summary

- Systems work together
- Humans affect the environment
- Protect nature

Geography – Term 3

📘 Climate

Climate is the long-term weather of a place.

📘 Weather

Daily conditions like rain, sun, wind.

📘 Natural Resources

Things from nature like water, soil, and minerals.

🧪 Practice

1. What is climate?
2. Name one natural resource
3. What is weather?

🧠 Summary

- Climate is long-term
- Weather changes daily
- Resources come from nature

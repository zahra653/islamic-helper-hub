📘 English – Comprehension

Read the passage carefully:

Omar loved playing soccer after school. One day, he saw a younger child sitting alone on the field. Instead of playing with his friends, Omar invited the child to join the game. The child smiled and happily played along. After the game, the child thanked Omar for including him. Omar felt proud of his kind action.

🧠 Questions

1. What did Omar enjoy doing?

2. Who did he see?

3. What did Omar do?

4. How did the child feel?

5. Why was Omar proud?

6. What quality did Omar show?

7. What lesson can we learn?

8. Would you do the same?

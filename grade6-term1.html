📘 Grade 6 – Term 1

📊 Mathematics: Whole Numbers & Order of Operations

In Grade 6, learners begin to work with much larger numbers than before. Whole numbers can now extend into thousands, hundreds of thousands, and even millions. Understanding place value is extremely important, as each digit has a value depending on its position.

Another key concept introduced is the order of operations, also known as BODMAS. This rule helps learners solve mathematical expressions correctly by following a specific order:

Brackets → Orders → Division → Multiplication → Addition → Subtraction

For example, in the expression 5 + 3 × 2, multiplication must be done before addition. Therefore: 5 + 6 = 11, not 16.

This rule ensures that everyone gets the same answer when solving problems.

📖 English: Comprehension and Language Skills

At this level, learners are expected to read longer passages and understand them more deeply. Comprehension questions are no longer just about finding answers in the text, but also about thinking critically.

There are three main types of questions:

Literal questions: Answers are directly in the text.
Inferential questions: Answers must be worked out using clues.
Evaluative questions: Learners give their own opinion based on the text.

Strong reading skills help learners perform well across all subjects.

🔬 Natural Sciences: Matter and Materials

Everything around us is made of matter. Matter exists in three main states:

Solids have a fixed shape.
Liquids flow and take the shape of their container.
Gases spread out and fill any space.

Matter can change from one state to another. These changes include melting, freezing, and evaporation. Understanding these changes helps learners understand how the world works.

🌍 Geography: Map Skills

Maps are tools used to represent real places. They help people understand directions and locations.

Key features of maps include:

Direction (North, South, East, West)
Scale (distance on the map)
Symbols (represent real objects)

Being able to read a map is an important life skill.

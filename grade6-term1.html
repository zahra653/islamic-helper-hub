📘 Grade 6 – Term 1

📊 Maths – Whole Numbers & Order of Operations

Whole numbers go beyond 1000 and include place value up to millions.

Order of operations (BODMAS): Brackets → Orders → Division → Multiplication → Addition → Subtraction

Example: 5 + 3 × 2 = 11 (not 16)

📖 English – Comprehension & Language

Learners read longer texts and answer: • Literal questions (facts) • Inferential questions (hidden meaning) • Opinion questions

Focus: • Paragraph structure • Vocabulary • Grammar

🔬 NST – Matter & Materials

Matter exists in 3 states: • Solid • Liquid • Gas

Changes: • Melting (solid → liquid) • Freezing (liquid → solid) • Evaporation (liquid → gas)

🌍 Geography – Map Skills

Maps include: • Direction (N, S, E, W) • Scale • Symbols

Understanding how to read a map is essential for navigation.

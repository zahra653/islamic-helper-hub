Grade 5 – Term 4 Notes

Maths – Term 4

📘 Revision

Review all key topics from the year: fractions, decimals, percentages, and algebra.

📘 Problem Solving

Apply maths skills to real-life situations.

📘 Data Handling

Reading charts, graphs, and tables.

📘 Word Problems

Use different maths skills together to solve problems.

🧪 Practice

1. What is 50% of 40?
2. Solve: x + 4 = 9
3. Read a simple bar graph and answer questions

🧠 Summary

- Revise all topics
- Apply skills in problems
- Understand data and graphs

English – Term 4

📘 Revision

Review grammar, comprehension, and writing skills.

📘 Essay Writing

Write longer pieces with clear structure (beginning, middle, end).

📘 Language Use

Correct punctuation, spelling, and sentence structure.

🧪 Practice

1. Write a short essay about your holiday
2. Correct: "i went to school"
3. Identify verbs in a sentence

🧠 Summary

- Write clearly
- Use correct grammar
- Structure your ideas

NST – Term 4

📘 Revision

Review energy, forces, systems, and environment.

📘 Matter and Materials

Understanding solids, liquids, and gases.

📘 Uses of Materials

Different materials are used for different purposes.

🧪 Practice

1. What are the 3 states of matter?
2. Give an example of a solid
3. Why do we use different materials?

🧠 Summary

- Matter has different states
- Materials have uses
- Revise all topics

Geography – Term 4

📘 Population

People living in an area.

📘 Resources

Natural resources like water, minerals, and land.

📘 Use of Resources

How people use resources in daily life.

🧪 Practice

1. What is population?
2. Name one natural resource
3. Why are resources important?

🧠 Summary

- People use resources
- Resources must be protected
- Understand population

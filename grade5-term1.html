Grade 5 – Term 1 Notes

Maths – Term 1

📘 Fractions

Fractions represent parts of a whole. Example: 1/2 means one part out of two equal parts.

📘 Equivalent Fractions

Fractions that have the same value. Example: 1/2 = 2/4 = 3/6

📘 Adding Fractions

Add fractions with the same denominator. Example: 1/5 + 2/5 = 3/5

📘 Word Problems

If you eat 2 slices out of 8: 2/8 = 1/4

🧪 Practice

1. 1/4 + 2/4 = ?
2. Write 2/4 as an equivalent fraction
3. If 3 out of 6 slices are eaten, what fraction?

🧠 Summary

- Fractions show parts of a whole
- Equivalent fractions are equal
- Add fractions with same denominator

English – Term 1

📘 Comprehension

Reading a passage and answering questions to show understanding.

📘 Grammar

Includes nouns, verbs, adjectives, and sentence structure.

📘 Vocabulary

Learning new words and meanings.

🧪 Practice

1. Identify the noun: "The dog runs."
2. What is a verb?
3. Use "happy" in a sentence.

🧠 Summary

- Read carefully
- Understand words
- Build strong sentences

NST – Term 1

📘 Energy

Energy is the ability to do work. Examples: heat, light, movement.

📘 Types of Energy

Kinetic (movement), potential (stored energy).

📘 Simple Machines

Tools that make work easier (lever, pulley).

🧪 Practice

1. Name 2 types of energy
2. What does a pulley do?
3. Give an example of movement energy

🧠 Summary

- Energy is used every day
- Machines make work easier

Geography – Term 1

📘 Maps

Maps show places using symbols.

📘 Directions

North, South, East, West help us find places.

📘 Symbols

Symbols represent real things (e.g., a tree symbol = forest).

🧪 Practice

1. What direction is opposite North?
2. What is a map?
3. Give one example of a symbol

🧠 Summary

- Maps help us understand places
- Directions guide movement

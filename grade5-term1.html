Example: 1/2 means one part out of two equal parts.

Steps:

2. Keep denominator
3. Simplify

📘 English – Comprehension

Read the passage carefully:

Ahmed woke up before sunrise and quietly got ready for school. He helped his younger sister pack her bag and made sure she had everything she needed. On the way to school, he noticed an elderly man struggling to carry his groceries. Without hesitation, Ahmed rushed over and offered to help him. The man smiled and thanked him for his kindness. Later that day, Ahmed’s teacher praised him for always showing good character and responsibility.

🧠 Questions

1. What time of day did Ahmed wake up?

2. Who did Ahmed help at home?

3. What problem did the elderly man have?

4. What did Ahmed do to help the man?

5. What does this story teach us about Ahmed?

6. Find a word that shows Ahmed is responsible.

7. Why do you think the teacher praised Ahmed?

8. What is one lesson you learned from this story?

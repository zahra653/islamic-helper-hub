📘 Maths – Decimals

Definition: Decimals represent parts of a whole.

Example: 0.5 = 1/2

Steps:
1. Line up decimal points
2. Add normally
3. Check place value

Common Mistake: Not lining up decimal points ❌

📘 English – Comprehension

Read the passage carefully:

Fatimah always tried her best at school. She listened carefully in class and completed her work on time. One day, her teacher gave the class a difficult task. While many learners struggled, Fatimah remained calm and worked through the problem step by step. At the end of the lesson, she helped her friends understand the work. Her teacher praised her for her patience and leadership.

🧠 Questions

1. What kind of learner was Fatimah?

2. What did she do when the work was difficult?

3. How did she help her classmates?

4. What did the teacher say about her?

5. What quality did Fatimah show?

6. Find a word that shows she is hardworking

7. Why did others struggle?

8. What lesson can we learn?

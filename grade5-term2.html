Grade 5 – Term 2 Notes

Maths – Term 2

📘 Decimals

Decimals represent parts of a whole using a decimal point. Example: 0.5 = 1/2

📘 Place Value

Each digit has a value depending on its place. Example: In 2.35, 2 = ones, 3 = tenths, 5 = hundredths.

📘 Adding Decimals

Line up decimal points before adding. Example: 1.2 + 0.5 = 1.7

📘 Word Problems

If an item costs R2.50 and you add R1.50, total = R4.00

🧪 Practice

1. What is 0.4 + 0.3?
2. Write 1/2 as a decimal
3. What is the value of 5 in 3.45?

🧠 Summary

- Decimals show parts of a whole
- Line up decimal points
- Understand place value

English – Term 2

📘 Paragraph Writing

A paragraph has a main idea and supporting sentences.

📘 Grammar

Focus on verbs, tenses, and punctuation.

📘 Reading Skills

Understand meaning, identify main ideas, and answer questions.

🧪 Practice

1. Write a short paragraph about your day
2. What is a verb?
3. Add punctuation: "i like apples"

🧠 Summary

- Use full sentences
- Check punctuation
- Stay on topic

NST – Term 2

📘 Forces

Forces push or pull objects.

📘 Types of Forces

Gravity, friction, and applied force.

📘 Effects of Forces

Forces can change speed, direction, or shape.

🧪 Practice

1. What is gravity?
2. Give an example of a push
3. What does friction do?

🧠 Summary

- Forces move objects
- Gravity pulls things down
- Friction slows movement
